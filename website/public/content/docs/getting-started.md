# Introduction to Synabit

Welcome to **Synabit**! This is the beginning of your journey toward a faster, more secure, and truly private digital brain.

Synabit is a modern, ultra-fast, local-first productivity workspace designed to unify your notes, tasks, calendar, and ideas into one seamless, cross-platform environment.

---

## What is Synabit?

In a world where most productivity apps force you into their cloud ecosystem, Synabit takes a radically different approach. We believe that your data belongs to you—and only you. 

With Synabit, everything you create is stored locally on your device inside an encrypted database. There is **zero telemetry**, **no forced cloud accounts**, and absolutely **no vendor lock-in**.

### Core Philosophy
- **Speed First:** Because everything is local, interactions are instantaneous. No waiting for spinners, no network latency when opening your vault.
- **Privacy by Default:** Your notes are encrypted at rest. We cannot read your data, even if we wanted to.
- **Interconnected Ideas:** Synabit acts as your digital brain, allowing you to link concepts, whiteboards, and tasks seamlessly.

## Key Features

- 📝 **All-in-One Workspace:** No more context switching. Manage deep-focus writing, quick fleeting ideas, tasks, and calendar from a single interface.
- 🔒 **100% Local & Private:** Your data stays on your device. Period.
- ⚡ **Serverless P2P Sync:** Keep your devices in sync seamlessly. Synabit uses custom peer-to-peer (P2P) technology to securely transfer your data across devices over your local area network (LAN) or via our Managed Sync Server—no central server required for the open-source tier.
- 🤖 **Local AI Powered:** Integrate with your local LLMs (like Ollama) to brainstorm, summarize, and assist you right inside your workspace without sending a single byte of your data to OpenAI or Claude.

## Why Local-First?

Cloud software is convenient, but it comes with a hidden cost: you don't truly own your workspace. If the servers go down, you lose access. If the company changes its pricing or shuts down, your data is held hostage.

Synabit is **Local-First**, meaning it works 100% offline. The cloud is strictly optional, used only as a bridge for syncing (if you choose to use our managed sync service) rather than the source of truth.
