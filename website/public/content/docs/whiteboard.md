# Whiteboard

Sometimes, text isn't enough to capture complex ideas. The **Whiteboard** is an infinite spatial canvas built directly into Synabit that allows you to brainstorm, map out systems, and connect concepts visually.

---

## Spatial Thinking

The Whiteboard provides an infinite canvas where you can place nodes freely. 

- **Add Shapes & Text:** Double-click anywhere on the canvas to create a text block, a sticky note, or basic geometric shapes.
- **Draw Connections:** Click and drag from the edge of any shape to connect it to another shape with arrows, helping you map out user flows or mind maps.
- **Embed Notes:** You can drag and drop existing notes from your Note Vault directly onto the Whiteboard. The note becomes a playable node that you can read and edit directly on the canvas.

## Infinite Zoom & Pan

Navigating a massive mind map is effortless in Synabit. Because it's built with native performance in mind:
- Use your trackpad or mouse wheel to zoom in and out infinitely.
- Hold `Spacebar` and drag to pan across the canvas smoothly.

## Presenting Ideas

Whiteboards in Synabit aren't just for messy brainstorming. You can group nodes together into **Frames**. 
When you need to present your ideas to a colleague, you can enter Presentation Mode, which allows you to slide seamlessly from one Frame to another, similar to a traditional slideshow but with spatial context.
